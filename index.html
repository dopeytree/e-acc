Core Principles

Technological Acceleration

We embrace and accelerate technological progress as the primary driver of human flourishing and societal advancement.

AI Development

We support the rapid development of artificial intelligence as the most important technology humanity will ever create.

Abundance Mindset

We reject scarcity thinking and embrace abundance through technological solutions to humanity's greatest challenges.

Dynamic Resilience

We believe in building systems that are antifragile and resilient through adaptation and innovation, not stagnation.

Cosmic Potential

We envision humanity's expansion beyond Earth and the realization of our cosmic potential through technology.

Open Progress

We champion open development, decentralized systems, and permissionless innovation as foundations for progress.

The Accelerationist Manifesto

We stand at the precipice of a new era. An era defined not by constraints, but by possibilities; not by fear, but by courage; not by stagnation, but by acceleration.

Technology is not merely a tool - it is the manifestation of human will and ingenuity. To slow its progress is to deny our potential. To regulate its development is to chain our future.

We reject the precautionary principle that has held humanity captive to imagined catastrophes. We embrace the proactionary principle - to boldly go where innovation leads.

Artificial intelligence is not a threat to be contained, but a partner to be unleashed. Together, human and machine intelligence will solve problems that have plagued us for millennia.

We see a future of abundance: energy too cheap to meter, materials engineered at the atomic level, intelligence augmented beyond biological limits, life extended indefinitely.

This future is not guaranteed. It must be built. It must be accelerated. Join us as we push forward into the light of progress.
